Paratha



Description

an unleavened Nepali wheat bread that is usually fried on a griddle


Ingredients

  • Potatoes aloo
  • Ghee or oil
  • Green chilies (finely chopped)
  • Fresh coriander leaves (cilantro) (chopped)
  • Red chili powder
  • Red chili powder/Cumin powder/Garam masala
  • Ginger (grated)
  • Lemon juice
  • butter
    •


Steps

  1. Boil and mash the potatoes, then mix them with chopped green chilies, coriander leaves, spices, and lemon juice.
  2. Combine wheat flour, water, and a pinch of salt to form a smooth dough. Divide it into small balls.
  3. Roll each ball, stuff with the potato mixture, and seal. Roll it out into a flat disc.
  4. Cook the paratha on a hot griddle with ghee or oil until golden brown on both sides. Serve hot with butter.
