Ramen



Description

In the city of Savoria, Hiro discovers a hidden ramen shop run by the enigmatic Master Ramen, a culinary sorcerer. Each bowl of ramen holds the essence of emotions and dreams, transporting diners to a mesmerizing dreamscape. With newfound friends, Hiro embarks on a quest to master the art of ramen and uncover the secrets of Ramenia. Amidst ancient rivalries and dark forces, they seek the legendary "Umami Fusion" to save both worlds. "Ramen Dreams: A Taste of Magic" is an enchanting journey celebrating the joy of cooking and the heartwarming connections forged through the power of food.




Ingredients

  • 2 packs of instant ramen noodles
  • 4 cups of water
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of ginger, grated
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 2 cups of chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 cup of sliced mushrooms
  • 1 cup of baby spinach
  • 2 boiled eggs (optional)
  • Sliced green onions, for garnish
  • Nori seaweed, for garnish
  • 1 tablespoon of sesame oil (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon of chili oil (optional)
    •


steps

  • In a large pot, bring the water to a boil. Add the instant ramen noodles and cook according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  • In a separate pot, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger, saut√© for a minute until fragrant.
  • Pour in the chicken or vegetable broth and soy sauce. Bring it to a simmer.
  • Add the sliced mushrooms and baby spinach to the broth. Let it cook for a few minutes until the vegetables are tender.
  • Divide the cooked ramen noodles into serving bowls.
  • Ladle the hot broth over the noodles, ensuring the mushrooms and spinach are distributed evenly.
  • If using boiled eggs, cut them in half and add one to each bowl.
  • Drizzle with sesame oil and chili oil for extra flavor (optional).
  • Garnish with sliced green onions and nori seaweed.
